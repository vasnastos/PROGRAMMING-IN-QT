Formulas used::

m--feet-->meter/0.3048.
m--inch=m/0.0254.
m--cm=m*100.
m--mm=m*1000.
m--yard=m/1.0936.

In the form Value you want to convert please enter a number and then select from the choosebox a type(m,mm,cm).
After that go to the second form and from the choose box select the conversion type and the textfield will be autofilled 
with the conversion number.
